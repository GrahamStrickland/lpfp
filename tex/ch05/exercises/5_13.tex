% Exercise 5.13

We have the following expression in \path{src/ch05/factorial.hs}:
\begin{verbatim}
fact :: Integer -> Integer
fact n = product [1..n]
\end{verbatim}
used to calculate $n!$. Evaluating this in \verb|ghci| results in the 
following:
\begin{verbatim}
ghci> fact 1
1
ghci> fact 2
2
ghci> fact 3
6
ghci> fact 4
24
ghci> fact 5
120
\end{verbatim}
